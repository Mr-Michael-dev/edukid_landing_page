About This Project

As a teacher, I've often struggled to find educational video content suitable for my kids without the worry of them being exposed to adult materials. That's why I'm excited about our platform, which offers a diverse collection of high-quality educational videos that are entirely safe for young viewers. I believe it can help parents and educators like me provide a secure and enriching learning experience for children and teenagers.

It was created as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School
